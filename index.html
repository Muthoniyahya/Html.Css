AkiraChix.

AkiraChix is an organization that offers scholarship to young women who are unable to further their studies.
An institution that focuses on computer science and the main goal is to introduce more females in the tech industry and empower them.
AkiraChix was founded in 2010 by a group of women who were passionate about changing the landscape of the technology field and creating a community
that supports,connects and inspires women in the tech space.These young women challenged the status quo by encouraging and facilitating more women
to take up careers in technology-related fields.
